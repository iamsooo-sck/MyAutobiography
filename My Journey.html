Hi, I’m Angelo Suan Galing-galing, and this is my story. Life, for me, is a series of lessons, triumphs, and defining moments. With every challenge and joy, I’ve grown into someone who loves music, dreams big, and finds inspiration in the people and world around me. I was born in Cebu City, a place where my childhood is etched in cherished memories. Surrounded by wonderful people, I was taught values like kindness, curiosity, and resilience. These early years shaped who I am and gave me the courage to explore the world with an open heart. Singing has always been a source of joy, and my admiration for K-pop music plays a huge part in my life. The artistry and dedication of K-pop groups inspire me every day, reminding me of the strength in hard work and creativity. Whether I’m listening to my favorite songs or learning about the journeys of these talented artists, K-pop fuels my dreams and ambitions. Beyond music, I enjoy watching movies, playing volleyball, and connecting with others. These activities allow me to express myself and continue discovering new facets of my personality. Becoming an adult has been no easy feat—it’s about learning to navigate life independently and figuring out what path to take. These challenges have taught me the importance of perseverance and resilience, helping me grow stronger and wiser. I’ve come to see each obstacle as an opportunity to learn and improve. Looking ahead, I’m determined to graduate from university and become the person I aspire to be. Whether it’s making an impact through creativity or inspiring others with my story, I dream of contributing to a world that values kindness, passion, and genuine connections. My journey is far from over, and I embrace the adventures that lie ahead. With gratitude for the lessons I’ve learned and excitement for the future, I remain dedicated to living a life filled with meaning, joy, and inspiration.
